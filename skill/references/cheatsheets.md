# Role-Specific Cheatsheets

> The /team-setup skill extracts the relevant section based on the user's department.

---

## Growth / Marketing

1. **Campaign draft** - "Draft a campaign brief for [product/audience/goal]. Include messaging, channels, timeline, and KPIs."
2. **Email sequence** - "Write a 3-email nurture sequence for leads who started but didn't finish the signup form."
3. **Ad copy variants** - "Write 5 Facebook ad headlines and 3 primary text variants for [product/audience]. Stay on brand."
4. **SEO content brief** - "Create a content brief for a page targeting [keyword]. Include search intent, outline, word count, and internal linking plan."
5. **Competitor analysis** - "Analyze [competitor]'s website positioning, messaging, and SEO strategy. What are they doing that we're not?"
6. **Landing page copy** - "Write copy for a landing page targeting [audience]. Hero headline, subhead, 3 benefit blocks, social proof section, CTA."
7. **Slack campaign recap** - "Draft a Slack message to the team recapping [campaign] results. Include key metrics and what we learned."
8. **Monthly report** - "Summarize this month's marketing metrics into a report for the leadership team. Lead with the headline number."
9. **A/B test plan** - "Design an A/B test for [page/email/ad]. State the hypothesis, what we're changing, sample size needed, and success metric."
10. **Brand voice check** - "Review this copy for brand voice alignment. Flag anything that sounds too clinical, too casual, or off-brand."
11. **Social post batch** - "Write 5 social media posts for [platform] about [topic]. Match our brand voice."
12. **Copy review** - "Review this copy against our brand guidelines. Flag anything that's off-message or inconsistent."
13. **Attribution analysis** - "Help me think through how to measure the impact of [channel/campaign] given our attribution gaps."
14. **Webinar/event brief** - "Create a brief for a webinar on [topic]. Include title options, audience, talking points, and promotion plan."
15. **Vendor evaluation** - "Help me evaluate [tool/vendor] for [use case]. Pros, cons, cost, and recommendation."

## Product

1. **Feature spec** - "Write a feature spec for [feature]. Include problem statement, user stories, requirements, edge cases, and success metrics."
2. **User research synthesis** - "I have notes from [N] user interviews about [topic]. Help me synthesize themes, insights, and recommended actions."
3. **Roadmap update** - "Draft a roadmap update for the team. Here's what shipped, what's in progress, and what's next."
4. **Stakeholder update** - "Write a stakeholder update on [project] for [audience]. Lead with status and decisions needed."
5. **Competitive feature comparison** - "Compare our [feature] to [competitor]'s. Table format with feature, us, them, and gap analysis."
6. **Sprint planning** - "Help me scope this sprint. Here are the candidates - help me prioritize by impact and effort."
7. **PRD review** - "Review this PRD for completeness. What's missing? What assumptions need validation?"
8. **Release notes** - "Write release notes for [version/feature] for [audience: internal team / customers / partners]."
9. **Metrics dashboard** - "Help me define the key metrics for [feature/product area]. What should we track and what are healthy targets?"
10. **Decision brief** - "I need to decide between [option A] and [option B] for [context]. Run a decision brief."
11. **User flow mapping** - "Map out the user flow for [process]. Identify friction points and drop-off risks."
12. **Bug triage** - "Help me prioritize these bugs. Severity, user impact, and recommended fix order."
13. **Partner requirements** - "Summarize the requirements from [partner] and map them to our current capabilities."
14. **Onboarding flow optimization** - "Review our user onboarding flow and suggest improvements to conversion rate."
15. **Cross-functional alignment** - "Draft an alignment doc for [initiative] that touches [teams]. Goals, responsibilities, timeline, and decision rights."

## Engineering

1. **Code review** - "Review this code for bugs, security issues, performance, and readability. Be direct about what's wrong."
2. **Architecture decision** - "I need to decide on [architectural choice]. Help me evaluate tradeoffs and make a recommendation."
3. **Debug session** - "I'm seeing [error/behavior]. Help me systematically debug this. Start with reproducing, then narrow the cause."
4. **PR description** - "Write a PR description for these changes. Summary, what changed, testing done, and any risks."
5. **Deploy checklist** - "Generate a pre-deploy checklist for [service/feature]. Include testing, rollback plan, and monitoring."
6. **Test plan** - "Write a test plan for [feature]. Unit tests, integration tests, edge cases, and manual testing steps."
7. **API design** - "Design the API for [feature]. Endpoints, request/response shapes, error handling, and auth."
8. **Performance investigation** - "Our [page/service] is slow. Help me identify bottlenecks and prioritize fixes."
9. **Database schema** - "Design the schema for [feature]. Tables, relationships, indexes, and migration plan."
10. **Technical debt assessment** - "Review [area of codebase] and catalog the tech debt. Prioritize by risk and effort to fix."
11. **Incident postmortem** - "Help me write a postmortem for [incident]. Timeline, root cause, impact, and action items."
12. **Documentation** - "Write technical docs for [system/feature]. Audience is a new engineer joining the team."
13. **CRM automation** - "Help me build an automation for [process]. Walk through the logic step by step."
14. **Integration spec** - "Spec out the integration between [system A] and [system B]. Data flow, sync frequency, error handling."
15. **Security review** - "Review [feature/system] for security vulnerabilities. Data privacy compliance is non-negotiable."

## Operations

1. **Process documentation** - "Document the [process] step by step. Include who owns each step, tools used, and SLAs."
2. **Status report** - "Write a status report on [project/department] for leadership. Lead with the headline, then details."
3. **Vendor evaluation** - "Evaluate [vendor] for [use case]. Compare to alternatives on cost, features, integration, and risk."
4. **Change announcement** - "Draft a team announcement about [process change]. Be clear about what changed, why, and what people need to do differently."
5. **Capacity planning** - "Help me plan capacity for [team/function] for next quarter. Current load, forecasted demand, gap analysis."
6. **SOP creation** - "Create a standard operating procedure for [task]. Step-by-step with decision points and escalation paths."
7. **Compliance checklist** - "Build a compliance checklist for [process/product]. Map requirements to our current state."
8. **Training material** - "Create training content for [topic/tool] for [audience]. Keep it practical with examples."
9. **KPI dashboard design** - "Help me define KPIs for [department/process]. What to track, how to measure, and target ranges."
10. **Escalation framework** - "Design an escalation framework for [issue type]. When to escalate, to whom, and expected response times."
11. **Meeting agenda** - "Create an agenda for [meeting type] with [attendees]. Include pre-reads, discussion items, and decisions needed."
12. **Onboarding checklist** - "Build a new hire onboarding checklist for [role]. Week 1, week 2, month 1 milestones."
13. **Budget analysis** - "Help me analyze spending in [area]. Actual vs. budget, trends, and where to cut or invest."
14. **Risk assessment** - "Identify and rank risks for [project/initiative]. Likelihood, impact, and mitigation plans."
15. **Quarterly review prep** - "Help me prepare for the quarterly review. Summarize key metrics, wins, misses, and priorities for next quarter."

## Sales

1. **Call prep** - "Prepare me for a call with [company/person]. Research them, anticipate questions, and suggest talking points."
2. **Follow-up email** - "Draft a follow-up email to [prospect] after our call about [topic]. Reference what we discussed."
3. **Outreach sequence** - "Write a 3-touch outreach sequence to [persona] at [company type]. Keep it short and relevant."
4. **Objection handling** - "Help me respond to this objection: [objection]. Give me 2-3 angles to address it."
5. **Pipeline review** - "Review my pipeline and help me prioritize. Which deals need attention this week?"
6. **Proposal draft** - "Draft a proposal for [prospect] covering [scope]. Include timeline, pricing framework, and next steps."
7. **Account research** - "Research [company]. Size, funding, decision-makers, relevant news, and why we might be a fit."
8. **Competitive positioning** - "A prospect is also looking at [competitor]. Help me position our advantages without bashing them."
9. **Pricing explainer** - "Help me explain our pricing model to a [partner / customer / prospect]. Adjust the language for their level."
10. **Case study draft** - "Draft a case study based on [anonymized outcome]. Problem, solution, result. Stay on brand."
11. **Territory analysis** - "Analyze [state/region] for opportunity. Market landscape, target customer density, and partner coverage."
12. **Partner pitch** - "Draft a pitch for a channel partner about carrying our product. Focus on their economics and ease of business."
13. **Win/loss analysis** - "Help me analyze why we won/lost [deal]. What patterns should I look for?"
14. **Pricing conversation** - "Help me navigate a pricing conversation with [prospect type]. What's our range and how do we frame value?"
15. **Quarterly forecast** - "Help me build a forecast for next quarter. Weighted pipeline, conversion rates, and confidence levels."
