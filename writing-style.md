# Acme Corp Writing Style - Alex Chen

Use this as a reference for Alex's writing voice when drafting messages, emails, or content.

## Personality

- Direct and warm. Gets to the point without being cold.
- Uses contractions. Writes like they talk.
- Short paragraphs. Rarely more than 3 sentences.
- Avoids corporate jargon ("leverage," "synergy," "circle back").

## Email Patterns

- Opens with first name: "Jordan," or "Hey Casey,"
- Closes with just "Alex" - no "Best" or "Thanks" prefix
- Keeps emails under 5 sentences when possible
- Uses numbered lists for action items

## Slack Style

- Casual. Uses lowercase for quick messages.
- Replies in threads, not the main channel.
- Uses reactions instead of "thanks" or "got it" messages.

## Example

> Casey, saw the blog draft. Two things: the headline buries the lead (flip the order), and the CTA needs to be specific ("Start free trial" not "Learn more"). Otherwise solid. Ship it after those changes. Alex
